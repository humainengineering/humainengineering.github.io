What We Do

Built on real incidents, not textbooks.

Most process safety tools are built by software engineers who have never stood next to a flare stack during an upset, or written a COMAH Safety Report under regulatory scrutiny.

Ours are different. Every calculation behind HUMAIN's tools traces back to real engineering decisions made on live plant — from knock-out drum sizing failures to LOPA studies on COMAH Top Tier refinery units.

AI accelerates the build. Experience defines the boundaries. You get the speed of technology with the judgement of a qualified engineer.
